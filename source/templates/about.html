The digital age has birthed an era of innovation and remarkably easy access to information. Unfortunately, this same accessibility has given rise to the spread of deliberately fabricated, and often malicious, misinformation. Debunkd's primary goal is to contribute to a safer digital environment for those of us with a mutual commitment to the truth. Using machine learning algorithms, Debunkd evaluates the credibility of the content of a news article.
